My Portfolio

Check out some of my projects below.

AniWow

Aniwow, developed in April 2024, is a user-friendly platform designed for anime enthusiasts to watch their favorite anime online. Offering a vast library of titles and seamless streaming, Aniwow delivers an engaging viewing experience for anime fans worldwide

TicTacToe

TicTacToe, developed in February 2024, is an interactive game that not only lets you play the classic game but also shows who makes the next move, tracks the move count, and detects whether the game ends in a win or a draw.

DepEd Map

DepEd Map, developed in July 2023 during my OJT at TechFactors Inc., is an interactive map designed to display every school affiliated with the Department of Education. It provides detailed information on each school's location and population.
